Go to [app.collony.ai](https://app.collony.ai) and sign in with one of three options:

- **Continue with Telegram**
- **Continue with Discord**
- **Continue with Google**

<Frame>
  ![Login](/images/login.png)
</Frame>

Any method works regardless of which platforms you plan to moderate. Once you're in, you connect your Telegram and Discord accounts separately from the [Projects](/projects) page.

## Link your Telegram account

To manage Telegram communities, link your Telegram account once from the Projects page:

1. Open [@CollonyBot](https://t.me/CollonyBot) on Telegram.
2. Send `/linkweb`.
3. Click the magic link the bot replies with - it expires after 5 minutes.

Once linked, your Telegram groups appear in the Projects page and the sidebar project switcher.

## Link your Discord account

To manage Discord servers, authorize the bot via the **Add to Discord** flow on the Projects page. This connects your Discord identity and grants the bot access to your server in one step.

## Switching between accounts

Once your Telegram and Discord accounts are linked to your collony login, you don't need to sign in again with each platform separately. All connected communities are available from the same dashboard session.
