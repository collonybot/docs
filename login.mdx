Go to [app.collony.ai](https://app.collony.ai) and sign in with one of three options:

- **Continue with Discord**
- **Continue with Google**
- **Continue with Telegram**

<Frame>
  ![Login](/images/login.png)
</Frame>

Any method works regardless of which platforms you plan to moderate. After signing in, link your Telegram and Discord accounts from your account settings to manage communities on each platform.

## Account settings

To link a platform, click your username in the top right corner to open your account settings, then click **Link with platform** for Telegram or Discord.

## Link Telegram

Open account settings and click **Link with Telegram**.

<Warning>
  If Telegram linking doesn't work because you don't have the Telegram app installed on desktop, you can link manually instead using the steps below.
</Warning>

Manual linking:

1. Open [@CollonyBot](https://t.me/CollonyBot) on Telegram.
2. Send `/linkweb`.
3. Click the magic link the bot replies with - it expires after 5 minutes.

Once linked, your Telegram groups appear in the Projects page and the sidebar project switcher.

## Link Discord

Either sign in with Discord directly, or open account settings and click **Link with Discord**. This connects your Discord identity so you can claim and manage projects where the bot is already installed.
