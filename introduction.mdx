collony monitors your Telegram and Discord communities around the clock - removing spam and scams in real time, answering member questions from your knowledge base, and surfacing trends your team would otherwise miss.

The dashboard is your control center. From here you configure the bot, review every moderation action, track community sentiment, and manage your team's access - across multiple projects and platforms from a single interface.

<Note>
  Need help or want to see collony in action? Join the community on [Telegram](https://t.me/collony_official) or [Discord](https://discord.gg/na6xcu7xV7).
</Note>
