# 🛡️ Content Moderation

Keep chats clean by moderating stickers, GIFs, and animations.

1. Open **Content Moderation**.

![Moderation settings on collony.ai](/public/Screenshot2025-09-22at09.33.07.png)

2. Enable/Disable **Whitelist-only** mode for:
   - Stickers
   - GIFs/Animations

<Tip>
  In whitelist-only mode, anything not approved is auto-removed and sent for admin review.
</Tip>
